Exhale: A motivational fitness planner for students

National University of Singapore (NUS)

  • Team: Group of four
  • Duration: 4 months
  • Tool: Figma

Project Overview


The aim of this project was to design and prototype interactions that address a specific problem, using a user-centric design methodology. The goal was to enhance our understanding of the interaction design process and apply it in practice. We chose to focus on the fact that many students struggle to establish and maintain a consistent exercise habit, despite their university providing a variety of different activities. This led to the development of the app Exhale, which aims to help and motivate students at NUS to develop a consistent exercise habit.



Problem Statement


Our user research identified four common issues for breaking put of an excercise habit or not starting one at all. These were: difficulty in finding activities, lack of available friends, struggles to stay consistent during stressful periods, and finding time in a tight schedule.

Design Process

This project utilized a user-centric design approach. We conducted user research and analyzed data to identify three user tasks. For each task, we created a scenario, storyboard, two personas, and individual wireframes and prototypes. We evaluated the individual work within the group and combined everything into one prototype in Figma. Finally, we conducted a summative evaluation with four users to measure efficiency, effectiveness, and overall satisfaction using the app. From this, we gained valuable insights into areas of improvement for our prototype.

Key User Tasks

1. Plan your activities

Users should be able to easily plan and update their workout schedule based on preexisting plans and stress levels.

2. Change your motivation

Users should be able to input their incentives for working out in order to be motivated when necessary.

3. Find Activities

Users should be able to find NUS sports activities based on interests and friends’ activities.

Result


Exhale is the app for NUS students to easily plan and update their workout schedules, input their motivations for working out, and find NUS sports activities based on their interests and friends' activities, making it easier for them to develop a consistent exercise habit and achieve their fitness goals.

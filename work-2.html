Connectify: A  smartphone application interface for remote collaboration

Chalmers University of Technology

  • Team: Group of five
  • Duration: 2 weeks
  • Tool: Figma

Project Overview


This poject aimed at designing a GUI for a smartphone application for remote collaboration. Connectify is an application for short meetings between students, designed with a playful touch. Though its purpose is for shorter meetings, the application should not discourage its users who attend a meeting over an extended period. The application lets users easily connect with each other and contains features such as the classic video meeting functions, homepage, break-out rooms, synchronous text messages, and video meeting customization.



Problem Statement


Online collaboration has become an integral part of many professionals' work life today. Collaborative tools, such as Zoom, facilitate much of the collaboration, and the trend towards remote work is expected to continue to reduce travel and its climate impact. Nevertheless, some usage problems persist with a lot of these tools, and there is room for envisioning even better collaborative tools for more effective online collaboration.



Design Process


The problem statement and requirements were given at the beginning of the project, so our work began with benchmarking various collaboration tools like Zoom, Teams, and Messenger. Next, we created wireframes and worked on the overall app design, including the color scheme and logo.

Result

Video meeting functions

Connectify is a video and audio call application with standard features. Additional functions are available under More Options (represented by three dots) in the top left corner of the meeting room. Users can modify their name, video background, screen sharing, and other settings here. A Hang-Up icon in the top right corner lets users exit calls anytime.

Homepage

The homepage showcases the user's favorite groups at the screen's top, succeeded by an All Calls list. Initiating a new meeting is achieved via the New Video Meeting icon in the top right. Ongoing meetings are marked by a Green Video icon. Adding a group to Favorites is done by tapping the Star symbol, but it remains in the All Calls list. Scrolling reveals the 15 latest calls.

Breakout Rooms

All available rooms are shown in a grid overview, with each break-out room displaying the number of participants. Each break-out room has a Join button, allowing for quick and easy participation. When clicking on one specific break-out room, a list of all participants will appear, with the possibility to scroll down if the participants exceed the screen real estate.

Synchronous Text Messages

In meetings, a chat function facilitates text communication among participants.The chat is accessible from the bottom menu and will be displayed as an overlay on the meeting view. Messages can be sent to a specific person or to everyone in the meeting. A scroll view is located on the top of the page to make it possible to choose a receiver for the message.

Video Meeting Customization

Users can customize participant layout by dragging/swiping to rearrange and using pinch gestures to resize.

For over 10 participants in a video meeting, an expandable canvas interface is employed. Users can swipe both vertically and horizontally to reveal all participants, with their video partly off-screen, indicating the hidden content

Bookit: A user interface Design for a resource booking website

Chalmers University of Technology

  • Team: Individual work
  • Duration: 3 weeks
  • Tool: Figma

Project Overview


The aim of this project was to design a resource booking website specifically for Chalmers University students and teachers. Bookit streamlines the process of reserving rooms and equipment, while also providing a filter option to search for available resources based on specific criteria such as time, date, and room or equipment type. Users can also conveniently book multiple rooms and equipment at once. Additionally, the website offers user-friendly features for easy creation, editing, and cancellation of bookings.



Problem Statement


Balancing automatic booking and manual control during the booking process is crucial. The system should assist users in finding available rooms and allow for quick booking, while also enabling manual adjustments to room selection. Proper controls and feedback are necessary for effective interaction between automatic algorithms and user-controlled selection.



Design Process


The problem statement and requirements were given at the beginning of the project, so my work began with benchmarking. I took inspiration from other booking resources such as TimeEdit and Airbnb, and created some wireframes. During the website design process, I also asked friends and family to try the prototype and give feedback on the overall design.

Result

Home Page

The website's home page will offer direct access to search and filter functionality, enabling users to easily find available resources that meet their specific needs even without logging in. However, to make new bookings and access current bookings, users must log in to the website.

My Bookings

On the "My Bookings" page, users can view their active bookings. They can toggle between week and day view in the calendar to get the desired overview. The page displays important information about the booking, including the date, location, time, status, and whether it is a shared or personal booking. Users can also easily edit or delete bookings.

Undo

When you delete a booking, a pop-up window will appear, remaining visible for 10 seconds. Within this timeframe, you have the option to undo the action, and the booking will be restored. Alternatively, you can simply close the pop-up directly without affecting the booking. This feature ensures a user-friendly experience, providing a safety net in case of accidental deletions.

Booking A Room

You can easily search for a booking by location, date, and equipment. Unavailable time slots are displayed in grey, making it clear when a room is not free. Hovering over a slot in the calendar changes its color and provides additional details about the date and time. When you select a slot, the calendar automatically switches to the booking window for a seamless user experience.

Filter Option

A filter option allows users to fine-tune their requirements. The filtered results are presented as various booking options that can be directly reserved. The feature also suggests different room combinations to provide more flexibility in selecting the most suitable booking arrangement.

Automatic Booking

Furthermore, the filter offers users the choice to set a repeat pattern for automatic bookings. If a repeat pattern is selected, and one of the dates is unavailable, the system will provide alternative suggestions for available bookings. Alternatively, users can edit the booking manually. This feature enhances convenience and flexibility in managing recurring reservations.

Shared booking

Once the booking is confirmed, users have the option to share it with their classmates, teachers, or TAs. When the booking is shared, the recipient receives a notification containing details about the date, time, and location. The recipient can choose to ignore the invitation by selecting "cancel" or add the booking to their own "My bookings" section, which will then be displayed in their calendar for easy reference. This feature facilitates seamless collaboration and ensures everyone involved stays informed about the scheduled events.
